About Me

Young Entrepreneur, Juhi Soni graduated from the Young Entrepreneurs Academy (YEA!), Chicago, IL. By the end of the Class Curriculum, Juhi Soni owned and operated a fully formed and functioning business, which was carried on after her graduation from the program. YEA! offered Juhi Soni a unique model that helped her bridge her business and academic communities together, while creating meaningful ties to the area with other successful businesses and students.
