SCOO+TLE (SCOOP IN A BOTTLE)

SCOOTLE is an innovated flexible bendable convoluted scoop that can go into a bottle of any size

REUSABLE

Scootle is a scoop that will extract left over product from a near empty lotion bottle

EASY TO USE & STORE

Take out your regular pump from your lotion bottle and turn SCOOTLE into a scoop

SAVES YOU TIME & MONEY

Don't get frustrated trying to get the last bit of lotion out of your favorite bottle

WASTE REDUCTION & RECYCLE

Most lotion/beauty bottles contain leftover product that is thrown in trash. The leftover product ends up in landfills causing hazardous chemicals to mix in with the environment. These bottles cannot be recycled and end up amounting to tons of waste. This also leads to many problems including causing impurities in drinking water. There needs to be a device that can extract leftover product from these bottles. Two major benefits of this are:
1. Plastic/glass bottles can be completely clean so they can be properly recycled.
2. Consumers can get more from their product in turn also saving money.
